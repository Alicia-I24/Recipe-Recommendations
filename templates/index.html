Recipe Recommendations Based on Grocery Store Promotions

Project Overview

The objective of this project is to develop a user-friendly application that recommends a recipe to users (from a database of over 8000 recipes from the Food Network) based on current grocery store promotion items (from Super C and Metro).

How Does It Work ?

The user will randomly generate 5 products that are currently on promotion at a grocery store (Super C or Metro). Then, based on the products generated, 5 different recipes from the Food Network will be recommended to the user.
